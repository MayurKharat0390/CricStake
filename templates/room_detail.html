Room Rulebook

How CricCoins are staked & won in {{ room.name }}

⚡ Core Rules

🎰

Place Stakes Before Match

All predictions must be placed before the match starts. Once the match is live, stakes are locked and cannot be changed.

💰

CricCoins Currency

Every player starts with 1,000 CricCoins. Coins are used to place bets and multiplied on correct predictions.

✅

Automatic Settlement

When the match ends, the platform automatically settles all predictions and credits winnings to your wallet.

🔥

Prediction Streaks

Correct predictions in a row build your streak. Longer streaks unlock exclusive badges and prestige.

⟲

Abandoned Match Refunds

If a match is abandoned or cancelled, all stakes are automatically refunded in full — no coins lost.
